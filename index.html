A Place to Pause & Savor

Nestled in the vibrant heart of La Paz on historic Calle Sagarnaga, Catch Your Breath Coffee Bar offers more than just exceptional coffee. We're a sanctuary where locals and travelers alike can escape the bustling streets and indulge in artisanal coffee, fresh pastries, and warm hospitality.

Our cozy atmosphere, combined with the rich flavors of Bolivia, creates the perfect setting to catch your breath and savor life's simple pleasures.
